Tracy, it's been an incredible journey with you over the past year. We've shared countless memories, laughed together, and supported each other through everything. Here's a look back at some of our favorite moments.

Our Timeline

Our First Date

Our first date was at Mandaloun Restaurant in Batroun for lunch. Afterward, we walked through the old city of Batroun, laughing about how hot it was and how we were both a bit shy and sweating. It was a day filled with fun and the start of something beautiful.

Our Trip to Paris

Our trip to Paris was our first time traveling together. We explored the charming streets, visited iconic landmarks, and indulged in delicious cuisine. The Eiffel Tower, lit up at night, was the perfect backdrop for our love. We had so much fun and created memories that will last a lifetime.

Our Trip to Disneyland

Disneyland was a magical experience. We enjoyed the thrilling rides, met our favorite characters, and felt like kids again. It was a day full of laughter and joy, creating magical memories together.

Our Trip to Strasbourg

Our trip to Strasbourg was like a fairy tale. The magical Christmas market, the stunning architecture, and the cozy cafes made our trip unforgettable. We wandered hand in hand, marveling at the beauty of the city, despite the cold weather. It was a truly magical experience.
